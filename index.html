Yaohui Wang 王耀晖

Ph.D. Inria

I am a Research Scientist at Shanghai AI Laboratory. My current research focuses on deep generative models for image/video synthesis.

I completed my PhD from Inria, STARS team, under the supervision of Antitza Dantcheva and Francois Bremond. Before that, I received a M.Sc degree in Machine Learning from Université Paris-Saclay, (ex Université Paris-Sud) in 2017 and a Bachelor's degree in Microelectronics Engineering from Xidian University, China in 2015. More details can be found in my resume.
